About the Lab

The Social Neuroscience and Network Lab (SNNLab) explores how human cognition and social structures interact, with a focus on empathy, autobiographical memory, and decision-making.

We combine neuroimaging, behavioral experiments, and network science to study how personal experiences shape social understanding and cooperative behavior.
