Yonsei Social Network & Neuroscience Lab

Department of Sociology, Yonsei University, Seoul, South Korea

At Yonsei SNN Lab, we study how social networks, the brain, and health are interrelated.

We focus on three main questions:
1. How do the social networks around us shape the human brain?
2. How does the brain enable complex human social networks?
3. How do the brain and social networks work together to affect our behavior and health?

To explore these questions, we lead several research projects such as KSHAP (Korean Social life, Health, and Aging Project), KSAH (Korean Study of Adolescent Health), and KCWIS (Korean Child Wellbeing Index Survey).

We take an interdisciplinary approach to integrate theory and methodology from medical sociology, social network analysis, social neuroscience, and machine learning.
