## 2026 KSHAP Data Fair

We had an opportunity to present KSHAP Data to the public during the KSHAP Data Fair as part of the 25th National Sociology Graduate Student Conference. The session covered the research goals of KSHAP, study design and data collection, key variables and dataset features, and example research applications.
